ABOUT

    I am a Doctoral candidate at the Department of Physics at Worcester Polytechnic Institute. My research focuses on the theoretical understanding of Non-Equilibrium Thermodynamics and on the emergence of Complexity. I use theory, computation, and experiments to understand pattern formation in systems that have been driven out-of-equilibrium.
